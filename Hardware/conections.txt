GPS module to RPI conections
============================


GPS Pin -----> RPI Pin
    VCC -----   1 (3.3V)
    GND -----   6 (GND)
    TXD -----  10 (UART_RXD GPIO15)
    RXD -----   8 (UART_TXD GPIO14)
    PPS -----  12 (GPIO18)

Trigger signal (probe connector)
============================

    GND  ----- 23 (GND)
    SIGNAL --- 25 (GPIO11)
